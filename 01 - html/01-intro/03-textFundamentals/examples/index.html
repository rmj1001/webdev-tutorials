The Scranton Strangler!

The scranton strangler has been found! He was caught breaking and entering into the home of Jim and Pam Halpert. The strangler seems to have worked with them in the past at the old Dunder-Mifflin office.

The strangler has been known to use the following tactics in the past to perform his crimes:

  • Break into homes
  • Stalk victims
  • Kidnap victims

The strangler will be arraigned on Monday.
